Brand Information & Styling Information

Brand Information

This company sells merchandise from the anime-art style game, Genshin Impact.

The merchandise can be anything from travel backpacks and clothing to keychains and artwork.

The targeted consumer base are generally students and players of the game, Genshin Impact.

This company's products are enticing to most players of the game due to the aesthetics of the products as well as the incentive of owning a merchandise relating to the game's fanbase

Styling Information

In the game, Genshin Impact, there are elements in the game with their respective colours (refer to the colour palette).

All characters from the game have their own corresponding elements, and the idea is to filter these characters by their elements and design the pages according to the colour of their elements.

For example, if a certain character's element is "pyro", then they can be found through the search filter of "Pyro" and that page will have #EF6363(Pyro colour) as the page's background colour.
